---
title: "How user authentication works in OpenPOS"
description: "OpenPOS uses username and password authentication with persistent sessions. You need a registered account to access the POS interface and manage orders."
---

OpenPOS requires a user account to access any part of the point-of-sale interface. The process is straightforward: register an account once, then log in whenever you need access. Your session stays active until you explicitly log out.

## Getting started

There are two steps to gain access to OpenPOS:

1. **Register** — create a new account with your name, email, username, and password.
2. **Log in** — sign in with your username and password to access the POS dashboard.

## Sessions

Once you log in, your session persists across page loads and browser tabs until you log out. To end your session, click the logout link in the navigation or visit `/users/logout` directly.

<Note>
  All POS routes require authentication. If you attempt to access the dashboard or any other protected page without being logged in, OpenPOS will redirect you to the login page.
</Note>
