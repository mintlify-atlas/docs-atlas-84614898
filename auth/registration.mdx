title: "Register a new user account to access OpenPOS"
description: "Register for OpenPOS by filling in your name, email, username, and password. After a successful registration, you are redirected to the login page."
---

Before you can use OpenPOS, you need to create a user account. Registration takes less than a minute and only requires a few basic details.

## Registration form

Navigate to `/users/register` to open the registration form. Fill in all five required fields:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Your display name |
| **Email** | A valid email address |
| **Username** | The username you will use to log in |
| **Password** | Choose a secure password |
| **Confirm Password** | Re-enter your password to confirm it |

## Validation rules

OpenPOS validates your input before creating the account:

- **Name** — required, cannot be blank
- **Email** — required, must be a properly formatted email address
- **Username** — required, cannot be blank
- **Password** — required, cannot be blank
- **Confirm Password** — must exactly match the value entered in **Password**

If any field fails validation, the form re-renders with error messages beside the affected fields. Correct the highlighted fields and submit again.

## How to register

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the registration page">
    Navigate to `/users/register` in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in your details">
    Enter your **Name**, **Email**, **Username**, **Password**, and **Confirm Password**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Click **Submit**. If all fields pass validation, your account is created immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in">
    You will see a success message — "You are registered and can now login" — and be redirected to the login page. Enter your new credentials to access the POS dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Passwords are hashed with bcrypt before being stored. OpenPOS never saves your plain-text password to the database.
</Note>
